How to Use the Control Center

1. Pending (Verification)

When an order arrives, it flashes orange. Click the image to verify payment. If valid, click "Approve Payment".

2. Assign Logistics

Once approved, select a Motorcycle or Bicycle to dispatch. You must hire couriers in the "Logistics" tab first.

3. Dispatched

The order is now en route. The customer's app will show it's on the way.

4. Delivered & Feedback

If the customer clicks "I Got It" on their app, the order turns Green here. If they send a message or click "It takes long", the card will alert you.
